IT DON'T MATTER — Artist Bio It Don't Matter is a high-energy 4-piece classic and southern rock outfit based in Gwinnett County, Georgia. Fronted by Lawrence's gritty acoustic drive and charged by Bruce's Texas SRV electric leads, the band brings a full-room experience every night. With a 30+ song repertoire spanning Stevie Ray Vaughan, CCR, Tom Petty, Lynyrd Skynyrd, and Drivin' N' Cryin', IDM specializes in packed, interactive sets where the crowd sings every word. Whether it's a packed bar, outdoor patio, or private event, It Don't Matter delivers the backyard-party energy that keeps fans coming back. Booking: Giggin' Management Team
